Travel to/from airport

If you are flying into Oslo you will arrive at and depart from Oslo Airport Gardermoen (OSL). Between the airport and Oslo Central Station (nicknamed Oslo S) there is a high speed airport shuttle, named Flytoget, running every 10 minute. The travel time is ~20 minutes. Departures can be checked here.

If you did not receive airport shuttle tickets before departure, please purchase one (ticket machines take credit cards) and give it to one of the organizers at any time during the conference and we will refund it in cash and give you a ticket for the return.

If your travel is delayed or if you have any problems during your travel, please let us know so we can assist you as good as possible.

Conference hotel

Your hotel is the Scandic Vulcan Hotel.

The address of the hotel is: Maridalsveien 13 A, 0175 Oslo and the phone number of the hotel is: +47 21 05 71 00.

Your room is booked in your name.

Getting there

It is possible to walk to the hotel from Oslo Central Station. It is about a 20 minute walk. This is the best and fastest walking route.

The best way to get to the hotel is by tram and then a small walk. From Oslo Central Station you can take tram 11, 12 and 13 eastbound to Schous Plass (four stops from Oslo Central Station). At Schous Plass you can walk a short route to the hotel. This map has the whole route.

Getting around in Oslo

Most of you will receive a public transport pass before you depart for Oslo. If you have not received one before departure, you will receive one when you check into the hotel. If there is not one for you at the hotel, please contact one of us and we will give you one.

The public transport pass is valid for 7 days from the day it's first validated. It is valid for all public busses, trams, subways and boats to the islands in the Oslo fjord and some local trains. You should validate the card each time you use the public transport system. One can validate the card on board busses, trams and boats. On subways and trains one need to validate the card before entering.

Timetables, network maps and all other information you should need about public transport in Oslo can be found here.

Speaker dinner

At 18:00 on the 1st of June there will be a speaker dinner. Organizers will be in the lobby of the hotel at 17:45 until 18:00 so you can join us there and we'll walk to the restaurant in a group.

If you have a special diet (vegan, vegeterian, allergies, etc.) pleace let us know in advance and we will arrange food which suit your diet.

After the speaker dinner there will be an social meet and greet where attendees can meet up and exchange their ticket for a lanyard etc. This will be at the V Bar og Bistro which is the bar on the ground floor of the conference hotel.

Venue

The venue is a dance performance venue named Dansens Hus and is ~30 meters (100 feet) from the conference hotel.

At the venue there will be a section of medium sized tables up front and chairs in an auditorium setting behind that. The speaker will be at the same level as the ground floor. There is no dedicated speaker area at the venue. For the speaker there will be a small round table in standing height and access to fresh drinking water.

Technical

The projector is a full HD 1080P projector with a 1920x1080 resolution. On stage there will be HDMI and VGA cable to connect to the projector. If your laptop needs a bridge to connect to HDMI or VGA, please bring one.

There will be a sound engineer controlling all sound from the stage and you will be given a small wireless headset microphone so your hands are free. If your presentation requires sound, please let us know before the conference starts so a cable to connect to the PA can be provided for you.

On stage there is also a dedicated internet connection which will not interfere with the connection used by the attendees. This connection can be used if you need a "better guarantee" connection to the internet (nothing is ever certain - have a backup plan just in case).

Norway use Type F power outlets. If your laptop has another type, please bring a converter.

Your talk

Please make yourself familiar with the schedule and the time for your talk. Please be by the stage about 15 minutes before it is your turn to enter the stage. Your speaker slot is 20 minutes.

We have a dedicated MC that will introduce you to the audience. The MC of 2016 is Jan Lehnardt.

We also encourage you to put your talk online after your talk.

Please note that everyone taking part in the NodeConf Oslo Conference must abide by the JSConf Code of Conduct.

Filming and streaming

Your talk will be professionally filmed and published on our video channel at Vimeo or Youtube as fast as possible after the conference. All videos published will be published under a CC BY-NC-SA 3.0 licence.

We will also stream all talks live in full HD. Information about that the conference will be streamed will be published the day before the conference.

If you do not want your talk to be filmed, published or streamed, please let us know up front.
